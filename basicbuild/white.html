Shades of Purity

Quiet Light

White is the whisper of untouched snow,


A gentle glow where soft winds blow.


It holds the light of morning skies,


And mirrors truth in open eyes.

A canvas waiting, calm and still,


Pure as hope, yet strong in will


White carries peace the world can feel.

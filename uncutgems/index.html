Welcome to Uncut Gems


Where a story is waiting to be cut...


Hi, I’m Izzy, the founder, designer, and diamond lover. I started this company because I’ve always believed diamonds should feel personal. I wanted people to have access to diamonds that are ethical, honest, and truly unique to them.

At Uncut Gems, you’re not just buying a ring; you’re part of it and the crafting process. From choosing your diamond to shaping your band, every detail is made with your story, and your style.

My mission is simple:
To create jewelry that feels real, raw, and original.

Our rings are crafted with care, using high-quality materials. Whether you’re looking for a bold statement piece or a subtle everyday piece, every ring is made with your story in mind.
